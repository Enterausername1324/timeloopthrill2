Who We Are

About Us

Our Story

Built on curiosity.
Driven by time.

Timeloop Games is an indie studio built on curiosity, creativity, and the belief that small teams can make big worlds. We craft unique, time-bending experiences that push storytelling into a new wave of imagination.

"Every project is driven by passion, experimentation, and the freedom to build games that feel personal — the kind you remember long after the credits roll."

Time isn't just a mechanic to us — it's the subject. We think about what it means for a player to experience a moment twice, to carry knowledge forward into a past that can't yet understand it. That tension between what is known and what is possible is where our best ideas live.

We're a small team. That means every decision has weight, and every game has intention behind it. There's no filler. No feature creep driven by committee. Just a clear vision, executed with care.
