Our Story

Built on curiosity.
Driven by time.

Timeloop Games is an indie studio built on curiosity, creativity, and the belief that small teams can make big worlds. We craft unique, time-bending experiences that push storytelling into a new wave of imagination.

"Every project is driven by passion, experimentation, and the freedom to build games that feel personal — the kind you remember long after the credits roll."

Time isn't just a mechanic to us — it's the subject. We think about what it means for a player to experience a moment twice, to carry knowledge forward into a past that can't yet understand it. That tension between what is known and what is possible is where our best ideas live.

We're a small team. That means every decision has weight, and every game has intention behind it. No filler. No feature creep driven by committee. Just a clear vision, executed with care and stubborn independence.

What We Stand For

Our Values

01 — Vision

Creative Design

We push boundaries in game design, blending narrative depth with innovative mechanics that surprise and reward players who pay attention.

02 — Identity

Time-Bending Worlds

Our signature style: games that play with time, memory, and the unexpected. Every mechanic has a reason. Every loop has a secret.

03 — Spirit

Indie Independence

Small team, big ambition. We protect creative freedom like it's the only resource we have — because it is.

04 — Craft

Intentional Making

We don't ship features. We ship feelings. Every decision is weighed against the player's experience, not a roadmap.

05 — Trust

Player Respect

We build games that trust the player to be smart, curious, and patient. No hand-holding. No condescension. Just play.

06 — Growth

Always Experimenting

Every game is a chance to learn something we didn't know before. Failure is part of the process — we fail loudly and fast.
